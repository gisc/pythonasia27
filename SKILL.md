# SKILL

## Programme vision

Python Asia 2027 should feel like a living system: technically serious, community-aware, and energised by the possibilities of AI-assisted development. The programme should create many entry points so that experienced engineers, researchers, educators, maintainers, students, organisers, and curious newcomers can all find a meaningful path through the event.

## Talks

Talks should combine substance with accessibility. We want sessions that show what Python is enabling now, what is changing next, and what communities need to stay thoughtful in an AI-shaped era.

Suggested talk areas:

- Python engineering at scale
- AI agents, orchestration, evaluation, and safety
- developer tools and automation
- data science and machine learning
- cloud, infrastructure, and platform engineering
- cybersecurity and resilience
- education, documentation, and community growth
- sustainability, accessibility, and responsible technology

## Workshops

Workshops should be practical, hands-on, and welcoming to different experience levels. Participants should leave with something they built, understood, or can immediately apply.

Suggested workshop formats:

- beginner Python foundations
- building AI agents with Python
- LLM application prototyping
- testing, packaging, and deployment
- data analysis and visualisation
- hardware, IoT, and robotics
- teaching Python effectively

## Engagement

Engagement should happen across formal and informal moments. The best conferences create spaces where people can ask questions, share work, meet collaborators, and discover new communities without feeling forced into high-pressure networking.

Possible engagement formats:

- hallway community demos
- lightning talks
- birds-of-a-feather sessions
- mentor office hours
- open source contribution corners
- newcomer orientation
- regional community meetups
- partner showcases with technical value

## Experience design

The programme should feel intentional from morning to evening:

- clear pathways for different attendee goals
- visible beginner-friendly options
- enough breaks for rest and conversation
- accessible rooms and instructions
- time for community recognition and volunteer appreciation
- moments that celebrate both regional diversity and shared technical curiosity

## Signature theme

The signature theme for Python Asia 2027 is the meeting point of Python, community, and AI agents. The programme should show not only what tools can do, but how people can collaborate with them responsibly, creatively, and inclusively.
