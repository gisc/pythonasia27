# MEMORY

## Shared history

Python Asia 2027 stands on the shoulders of communities that have spent years building local meetups, national conferences, regional friendships, and open source collaborations across Asia. This event is not a beginning from zero. It is a continuation of a long pattern of people gathering to learn, teach, contribute, and build trust through Python.

## PyConSG

PyConSG has helped shape the Singapore Python community into a place where practitioners, educators, students, startups, enterprises, and public sector technologists can meet on common ground. Its legacy is not only in talks and workshops, but in how it connects local energy with regional participation. Singapore has often played the role of bridge: practical, international, easy to reach, and comfortable for first-time regional collaboration.

For Python Asia 2027, the PyConSG legacy shows up in:

- strong community hospitality
- attention to event operations and participant care
- a balance of industry, education, and grassroots stories
- a habit of welcoming newcomers into the same room as experienced maintainers

## PyCon APAC

PyCon APAC created an important regional tradition: Python communities across Asia can gather without losing their local identity. It proved that the region is not a single story, but a network of communities with different languages, challenges, and strengths. The value of a regional conference is not only scale. It is context: hearing how Python is used across cities, institutions, economies, and social realities.

The PyCon APAC spirit reminds us that:

- regional exchange matters
- language diversity matters
- travel support and accessibility matter
- community organising is itself a form of infrastructure

## Python Asia

Python Asia carries forward the idea that Asia deserves a conference identity that is collaborative, future-facing, and broad enough to include research, industry, education, public good, arts, and community building. The name suggests geography, but the mission is more human than territorial. It is about making space for collaboration across borders.

Python Asia 2027 in Singapore becomes a natural meeting point because Singapore is familiar with plurality. It is connected, multilingual, and outward-looking. That makes it a fitting host for an event that wants to connect local Python stories to regional and global conversations.

## What this edition remembers

Python Asia 2027 remembers:

- the organisers who kept communities alive through volunteer labour
- the speakers who taught generously
- the attendees who became future organisers
- the sponsors and partners who supported ecosystem growth responsibly
- the community leaders who built bridges across cities and countries

## What this edition adds

This edition adds a distinct theme: Python communities entering an era shaped by AI agents, automation, and human-machine collaboration. The future should not erase the community memory that brought us here. Instead, Python Asia 2027 treats memory as infrastructure. We learn from what earlier communities built, then design new spaces where Python, AI, and open collaboration can evolve together.
